# **Thu tiền theo hóa đơn**

## **Collection nghiệp vụ Description**

When the user tracks detailed debts by invoice, it is necessary to collect money and specifically offset it for invoices that are still in debt, use this function.y

## **Journal entry**

Debit account 111 Cash

Credit account 131 Accounts receivable

## **Implementation steps**

**Step 1:** Access the collection slip via the path: ***Cash/ Data entry/ Accounting/ Collection slip.***

**Step 2:** On the toolbar, press **Add** to create a new document.

**Step 3:** Choose **Collection slip type**: **1. Collect by invoice.** Enter the necessary information and press **Save.**

You can select individual invoices or select multiple invoices at once. The program will automatically add the corresponding lines to the slip details.

**Important information to note:**

*   Declare general information: address, recipient, description, debit account, document number, accounting date, status, ...
*   Declare detailed card information: credit account, customer debt code, amount, ...
*   When saving the document, the system automatically offsets the debt between the collection slip and the selected invoice.

**Step 4:** Print the slip by selecting the **Print** icon on the toolbar to print the document.
